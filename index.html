A TASTE OF AUTHENTICITY

PENNE

Penne all'Arrabbiata is one of Rome's most beloved pasta dishes, known for its bold and fiery flavor. The word “arrabbiata” means angry in Italian, a playful reference to the heat of the chili peppers that give the sauce its kick.

TARTUFO AL CIOCCOLATO

A decadent Roman-inspired dessert, Tartufo al Cioccolato is a rich chocolate indulgence shaped like a truffle. Traditionally, it's crafted with layers of velvety chocolate gelato or mousse, often hiding a surprise heart of fruit or liqueur in the center.

PUNTARELLE ALLA ROMANA

A true taste of Roman tradition, Puntarelle alla Romana is a refreshing salad made from the tender shoots of chicory, finely cut into thin strips and curled in icy water to enhance their crispness.
